About Us

Our mission is to help brands get awareness while providing customers with a unique experience they wouldn't normally get.

Analytics

We provide a software solution for your company to get all the information it needs to make better decisions for your brand. Some common questions we get: how do we forecast demand? how do I know what products people want? will this new product actually work? These answers come from brick and mortar solutions and we provide you what you need to make the best decisions.

Awareness

We provide a strong marketing srategy for your company to get the audience it deserves by working directly with your marketing team. We have built our own brand to help promote your mission and message. Through tactics such as social media marketing, word of mouth strategies, and using our data, we will identify unique ways to help your end users find a ways to interact with your brand in person. We have a strong network of local influencers, companies, organizations, etc.

Easy Onboarding

Our team handles all the logistics to make this pop up experience as seamless as possible. From easy to understand lease and payment methods, to working with your team to set up things such as times, dates, etc. all you have to do is provide your products and we will do the rest!

Creative Suggestions

Our team prides ourselves on understanding the desired experiences of your end users using data and our history in the space. We will work with your user experience team to figure out the best design and experience for each and every individual. We utilize what your key demographic values such as posting on social media, emphasis on uniqueness, spontaneity, and more to create the best possible experience. Each experience gives us more data to make better recommendations.
